Leonardo Riolo’s Biography

Birth

I was born in Sardinia, a main Italian island in the centre of the Mediterranean sea. I was born in a small village called Lanusei on the 9th of August, 1997. My mother’s family comes from Sardinia, and my mother was there for holidays when I was born. My father’s name is Silvio, lawyer, and my mother’s name is Stefania, fashion agent.I also have a sister, named Valentina.

Early Life

My mother and father were living in Milan when I was born, so there is where I was raised. However, until age 5 when I started going to school, I spent a lot of time in Sardinia where my grandparents would take care of me. This is the reason for my big passion for the sea and the reason why I got so passionate about sailing.

Teenagerhood

As explained above, I was raised in Milan, where I attended all my primary and secondary studies. I attended a Scienfic high school, called Liceo Scienfico Cremona.During my high school studies, I also went in exchange to Australia, where I stayed for six months hosted by my host parents Fiona and Tony. During high school, I practised sailing at a competitive level, as part of the LNI sailing junior team.

University studies

Once I finished my high school in Milan, I chose to study business administration at IE University in Spain, Madrid. Here I studied a wide array of subjects, among which:

  1. Economics

  2. Finance

  3. Marketing
